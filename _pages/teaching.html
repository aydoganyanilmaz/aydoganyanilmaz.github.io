Let me tell you my philosophy of teaching: I love teaching!

Yes, as short as that! I simply love doing it. I get excited when I prepare a syllabus and do my best to pass that excitement to the classroom throughout the semester.

Experience

I taught a wide variety of courses in linguistics

After a good education in teaching during my undergraduate degree at METU FLE, I had opportunity to teach in a variety of settings in my professional career. I started as an English instructor at the Basic English School of Atilim University in Ankara, Turkey. Then, I started my master's at the Department of Linguistics at Hacettepe University where I was offered Research Assistantship. Due to the lack of enough faculty, I taught some undergraduate linguistics courses such as "Languages of the World" and "Morphology". Then, my teaching life continued in the US when I started my PhD at SBU where I taught a wide variey of Linguistics courses. Please check my resume for the full list. Also, I've been lucky enough to work with amazing teaching role models namely Richard Larson, John Bailyn and Nalan Büyükkantarcıoğlu
